APSC 101 MINING FEASIBILITY ASSESSMENT
APSC 101 Mining Feasibility Assessment
Year: 2026 Medium: Technical report, spreadsheet simulation, e-poster Role: Team Member (Constant-ine Consultants)

A group feasibility assessment for APSC 101 evaluating the viability of Copper-nicus Mining Ltd.'s proposed operation. Our team (Constant-ine Consultants) assessed water treatment system requirements, built a spreadsheet simulation to model treatment scenarios, and presented findings via an e-poster and technical report.

The project taught me how to scope a feasibility assessment when the variables are uncertain, and what a water treatment simulation needs to capture to be useful for decision-making rather than just technically accurate.
