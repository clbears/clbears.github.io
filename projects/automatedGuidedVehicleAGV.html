Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV)
Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV)
Date: November 2024

This project aimed to create an AGV capable of transporting payloads along a track with automated loading and unloading mechanisms. I served as the project manager and builder, contributing to the coding logic and mechanical assembly.

The AGV featured limit switches, a conveyor belt, and emergency stop functionality, showcasing my ability to integrate mechanical and programming elements.
