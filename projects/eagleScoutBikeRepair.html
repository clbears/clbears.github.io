EAGLE SCOUT BICYCLE REPAIR STATION
Eagle Scout Bicycle Repair Station
Year: 2024 Medium: Steel fabrication, concrete, community coordination Role: Project Lead

For my Eagle Scout project, I designed and built a public bicycle repair station. I secured donations of tools and materials from local businesses, led a team of 15+ volunteers through construction, and organized student workshops on basic bike maintenance.

The project required balancing engineering decisions (durability, weather resistance, tool security) with community needs (accessibility, clear signage, location). It was a crash course in stakeholder management—coordinating between the city, the troop, donors, and end users.

How do you secure tools in a public space while keeping them accessible?
What repairs does a cyclist most commonly need on the road?
