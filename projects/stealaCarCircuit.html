Steal a Car Circuit
Date: December 2023

This project involved creating a circuit where an LED lit up for a single unique 4-bit binary input combination. I contributed to designing the truth table, simulating the circuit in Multisim, and assembling it on a breadboard.

This project was an exercise in logical design and precision.
